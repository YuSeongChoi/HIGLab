📚 HIG Guidelines — 직접 체험하기
✓ Do — 올바른 예시
✗ Don't — 나쁜 예시

✓ 이렇게 하세요

  • Use a tab bar to organize content at the app level
  • Use SF Symbols for tab icons for consistency
  • Keep tab labels short (one or two words)
  • Use between 3 and 5 tabs
  • Use badges sparingly to indicate new content
  • Make every tab accessible from any other tab

✗ 이렇게 하지 마세요

  • Don't use more than 5 tabs on iPhone
  • Don't remove or disable tabs based on context
  • Don't use a tab bar for actions (use a toolbar instead)
  • Don't use overly complex or custom icons
  • Don't duplicate tab bar functionality in the content area
