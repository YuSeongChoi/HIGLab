# ``HIGAVFoundation``

A hands-on tutorial for building a polished camera app with AVFoundation.

@Metadata {
    @DisplayName("Building a Camera App with AVFoundation")

## Overview

Master the essentials of AVFoundation through a 10-step curriculum.
Learn by implementing the entire camera app process, from photo capture to video recording and real-time filters.

- **Chapters 1-2**: AVFoundation architecture and capture session
- **Chapters 3-5**: Photo capture, video recording, preview layer
- **Chapters 6-7**: Metadata capture and audio recording
- **Chapters 8-10**: Video editing, audio session, real-time filters
