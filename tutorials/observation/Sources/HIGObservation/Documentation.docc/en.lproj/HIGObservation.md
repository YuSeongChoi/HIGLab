# ``HIGObservation``

A hands-on tutorial for building a shopping cart app with the Swift 5.9 Observation framework.

@Metadata {
    @DisplayName("CartFlow: Mastering Observation")
}

## Overview

Master the core concepts of Observation through a 5-ring growth curriculum.
Build a fully functional shopping cart app called **CartFlow** while learning.

- **Ring 1**: Introduction to Observation & Basics
- **Ring 2**: Deep Understanding of @Observable
- **Ring 3**: View Update Optimization
- **Ring 4**: Advanced Patterns & Async
- **Ring 5**: Migration & Architecture
